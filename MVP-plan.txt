BirthdayBonus

This project will get birth months' deals based off a user's meals or beaty preference.
They will be able to select meals type or beaty type to get birthday/birth month deals from
various brands/commercials. Users are able to add new birthday deals to webpage if deals not 
there yet. 

MVP
-selecting meals and beaty to get appropriate deals on home page
-directing to "How it works" page in nav bar
-adding new deals to the list on new post page

Stretch Goals
-updating or deleting outdated deal lists

I personally love to keep an eye on coupons or deals on meals and beaty,
